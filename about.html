About

Best Countries Project was the brainchild of Prof. David Reibstein together with BAV and U.S. News who assessed 60 countries as if they were brands. The exercise gave insight into how various parts of the world view each country.

Inspired by the Best Countries Ranking research, Shenkar developed Nationlab to foster the creation of visual expression for each country. Students based their work on data presented within the research, alongside content collected independently.

Each student created a visual portrait of the country, while reflecting a specific category: Adventure, Open for Business, Influence, Citizenship, Entrepreneurship, Quality of Life, Power, Heritage and Movers.

Nationlab is designed to be an organic, multi-disciplinary exercise which can grow and evolve globally through the participation of other academic institutions.
